Why you’re still early — and what to do about it

The simple mental model for getting into crypto without fear of “being late”.

Key idea

Crypto moves in clear cycles. You don’t need to time the exact bottom — you need a plan that keeps you safe while you learn and gradually build exposure.

5‑min read

You’re still early if you see…

  • Headlines either doom or euphoria — both mean most people aren’t investing rationally yet.
  • Friends ask basic questions like “which wallet?” — adoption is not saturated.
  • On‑ramps and tools keep getting simpler — the next wave of users hasn’t arrived.

3 moves from Pickle

  • Set a tiny recurring buy (e.g., weekly). It trains discipline and ignores noise.
  • Open a self‑custody wallet and back up the seed safely. Practice small transfers.
  • Write a one‑page plan: what you buy, why, and when you stop.
