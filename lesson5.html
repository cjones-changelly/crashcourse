Why?

Bitcoin is the OG: it’s the most stable, the most trusted. It won’t 10x overnight, but it won’t vanish, either. It’s your anchor when things get choppy.

These are pegged to the US dollar, so they don’t swing wildly. Gives you flexibility if the market dips, and lets you “buy the dip”.
